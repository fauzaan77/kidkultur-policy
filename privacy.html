Kid Kultur Privacy Policy

Kid Kultur is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

  • What We Collect: Your mobile phone number when you sign up to receive OTP or authentication messages.
  • How We Use It: Your number is used strictly to send login verification messages. It will not be used for marketing unless you give separate consent.
  • Who We Share It With: No one. We do not sell, rent, or share your number.
  • Security: We use secure methods to store and transmit your data.
  • Contact: For questions, reach us at support@kidkultur.com.
