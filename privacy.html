Kid Kultur Privacy Policy

Kid Kultur (a service of Prudence Inc) is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our mobile app and services.

  • What We Collect: Your mobile phone number when you sign up to receive OTP or authentication messages.
  • How We Collect It: Information is collected when you input your phone number into our app to request OTP verification.
  • How We Use It: Your number is used strictly to send login verification messages. It is never used for marketing unless you provide separate consent.
  • Who We Share It With: We do not sell, rent, or share your number with any third parties.
  • Security: We use encryption and access control to protect your personal information and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Your Rights: You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data.
  • Carrier Disclaimer: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Contact: For any privacy-related inquiries, email us at cs@prudenceinc.com.
